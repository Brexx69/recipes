Lasagna

The lasagna is a delicious Italian plate, it is common for the grandmothers' cliche' that they always cook it.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses

Steps

  1. 1) Make the meat sauce.
  2. 2) Cook the noodles.
  3. 3) Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. 4) Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. 5) Cover with foil and bake.
  6. 6) Let the lasagna rest before serving.
